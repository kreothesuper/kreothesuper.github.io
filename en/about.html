About us

GHS is a team of professionals that has been established since 10 years and includes specialists in various fields — from veterinarians to riders.

With many years of experience in the equestrian market, GHS helps its clients to select proven sports horses in various disciplines around the world.

The Concierge service dealing with everything from your personal travel arrangements and horse transportation to providing expert opinion of leading equestrian professionals.

Our platform acts as your personal assistant and guide to the global horse market, introducing you to all the nuances and protecting you from unpleasant situations. You no longer need to look for trusted people, collect information from a variety of sources and think about the comfort of your trip – We will do it for you!

GHS will take care of all organizational matters, providing you with maximum convenience and comfort.
