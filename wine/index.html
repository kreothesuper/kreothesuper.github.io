SUPPLY DYNAMICS:
THE STATES OF WINE

$WINE introduces a unique and engagung game mechanic inspired by the states of wine - staking (icing) and burning (vaporizing)

FREEZING (STAKING)

Just like grapes turn into wine when fermented, $WINE tokens can be staked (frozen) to earn rewards. Stakers are rewarded with additional $WINE tokens over time, simulating the process of accumulating grapes

MECHANISM

Users stake $WINE tokens for a specified period.

The longer the staking period, the higher the rewards.

Staking rewards are distributed from the community rewards pool.

4.5% total allocated for a year between 3 pools: daily, weekly, and monthly

At 20% of the presale tokens evenly staked to these pools, users will get 50% APR

APR = % TOTAL STAKING REWARDS / % OF TOTAL SUPPLY

VAPORIZING (BURNING)

When the grapes begin to ferment, they turn into delicious wine and disappear. Likewise, $WINE tokens can be burned (evaporated) to reduce the total supply, increasing the scarcity and possibly the value of the remaining tokens.

MECHANISM

Users can send $WINE tokens to burn contract

Every week, 25% of these tokens burned, 25% sent to charity, and 50% are rained (raffled) among all participants giving them back from 0.1x to 10x of tokens they burned (with 2 times higher odds for getting less than 1x)
