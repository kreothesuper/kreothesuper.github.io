Informed Consent Development

This applies to all research activities involving human subjects whether that research is done as part of a drug trial or investigator initiated research. Informed Consent Development will be based on the local regulations, ICH GCP and protocol design. ICF involves providing a potential participant with: adequate information to allow for an informed decision about participation in the clinical investigation. The informed Consent Form will include the purpose, duration, risks, benefits, costs and additional expenses of the trial; a description of the trial procedures and number of visits at the site; alternative care options; and volunteers’ rights.

Patient recruitment services

Many clinical trials run into the problem - a lack of participants in clinical studies. This in turn extends the duration of the trial or leads to an increase in the number of clinical trial sites, thus increasing costs for the Sponsor. R4you company is devoted to optimize the Recruitment of patients for clinical studies. At R4you company, we have experience using our innovative platform to bring patients to a variety of studies. Our company suggests patient enrolment services. We are working parallel to the site's recruitment and refer only relevant inquiries from the subjects directly to the Study Coordinators. In addition, we are building digital campaigns for patient recruitment (Facebook/Google) in every country in every language; and we recruit the subjects also by our large Patient’s Database, which includes more than 60,000 potential subjects! We can focus advertising according next criteria’s: age/ gender/ geographical region/ disease and more, so only this specific population will see an advertisement.
